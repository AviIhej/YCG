York Consulting Group (YCG) is a student-run management consulting firm affiliated with the Schulich School of Business that specializes in addressing the strategy, marketing, finance, and process needs of small- and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

Our consulting team provides affordable, top-tier advisory services in the areas of strategy, marketing, finance, human capital, operations, and technology among other practices.

As the largest business school-consulting firm in Canada, YCG has developed a significant base of expertise and breadth of industry knowledge. This directly benefits our clients as cross-functional teams are assigned to projects that best match their requirements.

WHY YCG

We recognize that small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) form the backbone of the Canadian economy, and that their success is vital to our country’s prosperity.

In this time of growing economic uncertainty, unprecedented technological disruption, and ever-increasing globalized competition, Canadian businesses require deep, practical insights into the issues that most impact their bottom line.

Our organization is uniquely positioned to assist SMEs with a broad range of business challenges, delivering effective, tailored solutions that help clients achieve sustainable competitive advantage in their industry.

YCG comprises a select group of MBA, MMgt, and JD/MBA candidates, offering years of industry experience in financial services, healthcare research, marketing, operations management, project management, IT service management, and human capital, among other fields.

Our consultants also have access to a wealth of informational resources through York University, including the library portals, proprietary database subscriptions, and expert faculty. Leveraging the resources and expertise at our disposal, YCG provides clients with innovative, data-driven solutions to achieve meaningful outcomes and capture lasting advantage.
